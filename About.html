How to Play

Rules

01 Click Start Game to begin. A sequence of colors will flash on the screen.
02 Watch carefully and memorize the exact order in which the colors blink.
03 Once it's Your Turn!, click the colored buttons in the same order.
04 If you get it right, you advance to the next level — with one more color added to the sequence!
05 Click a wrong color and it's Game Over. The game resets and you can try again.
